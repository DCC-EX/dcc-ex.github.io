Supported Microcontrollers Setup Notes
***************************************

These pages are notes and guides for how to connect and configure each specific microcontroller supported by DCC++ EX. Click on the microcontroller you want to use. Be sure to read the :doc:`Getting Started and Assembly sections </get-started/index>` as well. For a listing of the different types of microcontroller boards and whether they are supported or not, see the :doc:`Microcontroller Boards </reference/hardware/microcontroller-boards>` page.
