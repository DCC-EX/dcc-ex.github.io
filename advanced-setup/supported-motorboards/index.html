Supported Motorboards Setup Notes¶

These pages are notes and guides for how to connect and configure each specific motor controller supported by DCC++ EX. Click on the motor controler you want to use. Be sure to read Motor Board Config as well. For a listing of the different types of motor boards and whether they are supported or not, see the Motor Boards page.
