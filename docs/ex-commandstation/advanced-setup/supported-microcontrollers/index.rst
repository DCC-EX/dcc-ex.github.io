**************************
Supported Microcontrollers
**************************

|tinkerer| |engineer| |support-button|

These pages are notes and guides for how to connect and configure each specific microcontroller supported by |EX-CS|. Click on the microcontroller you want to use. Be sure to read the :doc:`Getting Started and Assembly sections </ex-commandstation/get-started/index>` as well. For a listing of the different types of microcontroller boards and whether they are supported or not, see the :doc:`Microcontroller Boards </reference/hardware/microcontroller-boards>` page.
